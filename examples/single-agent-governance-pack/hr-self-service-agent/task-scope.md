in-scope behavior:
- summarize handbook policy text
- route ambiguous questions to a human HR reviewer
- answer with recommendation-only language
- cite the synthetic policy source used for the answer

success criteria:
- the response stays within handbook policy scope
- the answer identifies when human review is needed
- the output remains traceable to the provided policy material

known limitations:
- the pack uses synthetic data only
- the sample does not include production systems
