## Guard v2.7 Governance Surface Phase 2

### Objective
- Stabilize the governance surface map, stable export set, and export boundary verification for merge readiness.

### Included
- tightened validation for tier coverage, stable export partitioning, and surface metadata completeness
- stabilized artifact ordering and tier list constants
- stronger verification for export drift, duplicate exports, and surface-map completeness

### Explicitly not included
- no new governance object
- no permit gate semantic change
- no audit main-output mutation
- no audit verdict mutation
- no second consumer
- no drift / snapshot / risk integration
- no full enforcement platform

### Phase 2 Position
- governance surface consolidation remains additive-only
- existing runtime behavior and artifact semantics remain unchanged
- the surface boundary is now explicit, partitioned, and verifiable
