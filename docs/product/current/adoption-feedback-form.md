## Evidence Pack Feedback

- Was the Evidence Pack easy to fill?
- Could you understand what the workflow is allowed to do?
- Could you understand what the workflow is not allowed to do?
- What evidence was missing?

## Report Feedback

- Was the JSON report useful?
- Which JSON report sections were most useful?
- Which report sections were unclear?

## Handoff Feedback

- Was the Markdown handoff useful?
- Did the packet feel ready to forward to another reviewer?
- Would you consider this review evidence, not approval evidence?

## Security / Redaction Feedback

- Was sensitive material easy to redact or summarize?
- Did any requested evidence feel unsafe to share?
- Which evidence needed synthetic, redacted, or summary treatment?

## Adoption Intent

- Would your team reuse this for another workflow?
- What would block adoption?
- What would make a second trial easier?

## Missing Capabilities

- What evidence, explanation, or handoff material was missing?
- What capability would you want next before broader reuse?

## Follow-up Questions

- What should the team inspect next?
- Which workflow should be trialed next?
- Who owns the next follow-up?

## Boundary

This form captures adoption feedback only. The human review decision remains outside Guard.

MindForge Guard does not approve, block, deploy, certify, guarantee legal compliance, or control execution.
